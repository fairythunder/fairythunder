I'm busy

Download

Introduction

Not enough live news on Youtube? Want to change channel when you encounter an advertisement? Although all major browsers currently have Tab function, you can open many pages to view, but it is very troublesome to ofent switch. If you open multiple browser windows to view, you will need to re-adjust the window position next time after you close it; If you use extended plugin (such as Dualless, Tab Resize-split screen layouts) to reset the layout, but still feel a little insufficient. I'm busy is a browser based on Microsoft Edge (Chromium). It provides a choice for people who want to watch multiple video and audio channels at a time. You can watch as much as you want at the same time, as busy as you want!

Introduction

Letters Collector is a little game that help Chinese users remember English words. Letters Collector will randomly display letters on the screen. Player must collect the letters in order to get the correct words & scores. If word prompt is displayed, player will not get the score. The wrong collection order will decrease the score. Come and try to see how much score you cant get!
